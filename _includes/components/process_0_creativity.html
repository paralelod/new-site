Design & Creativity

I use design thinking and creative problem solving methods to help business, individuals and communities to explore and embrace new ideas.

Creative Problem Solving

Creative Problem Solving, is an approach to a problem or challenge in an “out of the box” way. It helps (re)frame problems and opportunities, come up with meaningful findings and actionable insights.

Design Thinking

Design thinking, employs divergent thinking as a way to ensure that many possible solutions are explored in the first instance, and then convergent thinking as a way to narrow these down to a final solution.
